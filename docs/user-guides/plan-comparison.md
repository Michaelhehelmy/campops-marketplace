## Plan Details

### Basic Plan

Ideal for new properties getting started on the platform.

**What's included:**

- Property listing with public search visibility
- Online booking engine
- All available plugins
- Staff management (up to 5)
- Housekeeping and maintenance modules
- Standard commission rate (15%)
- Platform subdomain

**What's NOT included:**

- Room & unit management — cannot modify room inventory or pricing
- Guest CRM — limited guest lookup
- Financial reports — no revenue analytics
- Settings access — cannot modify property settings
- Plugin configuration — cannot install/configure plugins
- Custom domain — subdomain only
- Priority support

### Premium Plan

Best for growing properties that need more control.

**What's included:**

- Everything in Basic, plus:
- Room management — create and price room types
- Guest CRM — full guest profiles and history
- Financial reports — revenue, commissions, payouts
- Settings access — configure property details and domain
- Plugin management — install and configure plugins
- Up to 20 staff members
- Reduced commission rate (12.5%)
- Priority support

### Ultimate Plan

For established properties that want full control and a branded experience.

**What's included:**

- Everything in Premium, plus:
- **Custom domain support** — use your own domain (e.g., `yourproperty.com`)
- Unlimited staff members
- Lowest commission rate (10%)
- Highest priority support
- Full platform customization

## Plugin Availability

All plugins are available on all plan tiers. The key difference is **who can configure them**:

| Plan         | Can Install Plugins? | Can Configure Plugins? |
| ------------ | -------------------- | ---------------------- |
| **Basic**    | No (pre-installed)   | No                     |
| **Premium**  | Yes                  | Yes                    |
| **Ultimate** | Yes                  | Yes                    |

Available plugin categories:

| Category           | Plugins                                               |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Commerce**       | Booking engine, POS/KDS, Subscriptions                |
| **Operations**     | Housekeeping, Maintenance, Resource management        |
| **Distribution**   | iCal Sync, iCal Import, Channel Manager, Siteminder   |
| **Marketing**      | CRM, Guest CRM, Marketing Automation, Loyalty program |
| **Finance**        | Accounting, Financial Operations, Inventory Waste     |
| **HR**             | HR Core, Staff Roster                                 |
| **Admin**          | Listing Admin, Owner dashboard, Activities            |
| **Infrastructure** | PWA, Test Dock                                        |

## Commission Structure

| Plan         | Base Commission Rate | Example (per $100 booking)              |
| ------------ | -------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| **Basic**    | 15%                  | $15.00 platform fee → $85.00 net to you |
| **Premium**  | 12.5%                | $12.50 platform fee → $87.50 net to you |
| **Ultimate** | 10%                  | $10.00 platform fee → $90.00 net to you |

Commission is calculated on the total booking amount before taxes. Rates are configurable per-property and may vary based on agreements.

## Custom Domain (Ultimate Only)

With the Ultimate plan, you can use your own domain:

1. Navigate to **My Property** → **Domain** section.
2. Enter your domain name.
3. Configure DNS: Add a CNAME record pointing to the platform.
4. Verify the domain through the settings page.
5. Once verified, your property is accessible at your domain.

**Benefits of a custom domain:**

- Branded experience for guests
- Better search engine visibility
- Professional appearance
- Guests book directly on your domain

## Upgrading Your Plan

### How to Upgrade

1. Log in to your property dashboard.
2. Click the **Upgrade** link next to your current plan label.
3. Select the desired plan tier.
4. Confirm the upgrade.

Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll have access to new features right away.

### What Changes

When you upgrade from **Basic to Premium**:

- Room management becomes available
- Guest CRM unlocks full profiles
- Financial reports appear
- Settings and plugin configuration open up

When you upgrade from **Premium to Ultimate**:

- Custom domain setup becomes available
- Commission rate drops to 10%
- Unlimited staff slots
- Priority support

---

## Downgrading

### How to Downgrade

Contact platform support to request a downgrade. Downgrades are not available via self-service.

### What Happens When You Downgrade

**Premium → Basic:**

- Room management is hidden
- Guest CRM reverts to limited view
- Financial reports are hidden
- Settings and plugin configuration are locked
- Staff count limited to 5

**Ultimate → Premium:**

- Custom domain stops working
- Commission rate increases to 12.5%
- Staff limited to 20

**Important:** Downgrading does not delete your data. If you upgrade again later, your previous configuration is restored.
